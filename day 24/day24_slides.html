Coming Up Next: Video Editing!

Why Learn Video Editing?

Video is EVERYWHERE - TikTok, YouTube, Instagram Reels, job applications, school projects. Knowing how to edit video is a seriously valuable skill!

What You'll Learn:

  • Cutting & Trimming - Remove the boring parts
  • Transitions - Smooth cuts between clips
  • Text & Titles - Add captions and graphics
  • Audio - Background music and sound effects
  • Effects & Filters - Make it look professional
  • Exporting - Get it ready to share
Did You Know? The average TikTok creator spends 30-60 minutes editing a single 60-second video. Those quick cuts and perfect timing don't happen by accident!

Timeline-based editing
Stock videos & music library
Screen & webcam recording
Text-to-speech
Auto captions
Green screen effects
Export to 1080p (free)
Cloud sync with Microsoft
How to Open: Search "ClipChamp" in the Windows Start menu, or go to clipchamp.com in your browser!
